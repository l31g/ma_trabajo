# Planned vacation to protect

## Vacation dates

- **September 18 through October 3, 2026**
- Approximate workdays for a Monday-Friday schedule: **11 workdays / 88 hours**

## Why this needs special attention

This vacation was approved before the JHHS transition announcement. Because JHU vacation/PTO is expected to be paid out at transfer instead of carrying into JHHS, the main risk is not the JHU balance itself. The main risk is whether JHHS will honor the already-approved dates after the transfer.

## Evidence to save

- Original vacation approval email, screenshot, or system record.
- Date the vacation was approved.
- Name/title of the approving manager.
- Any calendar entry, travel booking, or related confirmation showing the dates were planned before the transition announcement.
- Any later HR or manager message confirming JHHS will honor the dates.

## Questions to ask HR or the JHHS placement contact

1. This vacation was approved before the JHHS transition announcement. Will JHHS honor the already-approved September 18-October 3, 2026 vacation dates?
2. If my JHU vacation balance is paid out at transfer and I do not yet have enough JHHS PTO accrued, how will these dates be handled?
3. Can the time be approved as unpaid leave, advanced PTO, a transition exception, or another approved absence?
4. Will taking this pre-approved vacation affect my 90-day probationary period, benefits, or employment status?
5. Can HR or my JHHS manager confirm the answer in writing before I accept the offer?
