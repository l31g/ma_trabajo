Use `jhu-jhhs-transfer-notes.md` as the current source of repo context. It contains the prior agent's notes, including the main concerns, public source found so far, likely PTO and retirement questions, and documents to request or save.

- JHU and JHHS are separate organizations.

- The prior notes suggest years of service should be recognized across JHU and JHHS, but the exact benefit effects need confirmation from official documents.
- The prior notes suggest accrued JHU vacation/PTO may be paid out by JHU at transfer instead of carrying over, but this must be verified against actual HR materials.

## Agent guidelines

- Treat screenshots, benefits records, pay details, employee IDs, account numbers, addresses, and contact details as sensitive personal information.
- Do not upload, paste, or summarize sensitive documents into third-party systems.
- Do not invent policy answers. Mark unconfirmed claims clearly and prefer direct quotes from official HR or plan documents.
- When researching public sources, save the URL, page title, publication or effective date, access date, and a short quote or summary.
- When summarizing screenshots or documents, include the document date, screenshot date if known, and what question the evidence answers.
- Keep edits factual and neutral. This repo is for organization and evidence, not legal advice.
