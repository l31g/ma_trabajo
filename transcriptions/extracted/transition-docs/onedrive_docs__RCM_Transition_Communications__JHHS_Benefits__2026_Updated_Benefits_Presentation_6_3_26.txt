Flexible Spending Accounts (FSA’s)
An FSA is a flexible spending account designed to allow individuals to
put away tax-free dollars for either healthcare expenses or daycare
expenses. It’s a use it or lose it type benefit.
• Enroll in Smart source
• Contribute biweekly through payroll deductions
• Use debit card (Health Care FSA only) and/or submit claims for
reimbursement
• Maximum IRS 2026 Contributions are:

- Healthcare FSA  $3,400 (up to $680 rollover)

- Dependent Care FSA $5,000 (no rollover)

Disability Insurance
• Short Term Disability
o Company paid benefit
o 7 calendar day elimination period
o 60% of pay for up to 25 weeks
• Long Term Disability
o Automatic enrollment unless you waive when making elections
o Requires employee contribution
o 60% of salary (non-taxable) starting after 26 weeks of disability up to a
maximum of $10,000 per month.
